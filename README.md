## What is YourYear?

YourYear is a **local-first goal tracking app** that helps you turn big ambitions into daily progress. No sign-up, no cloud sync, no tracking — just you and your goals.

```
Learn a new language   ████████████░░░░░░░░   60%
Run a marathon         █████░░░░░░░░░░░░░░░   25%
Read 24 books          ████████████████████  100% ✓
```

---

## Features

### 🎯 Centralize Your Goals
Keep all your goals in one place. See everything at a glance — no more scattered sticky notes or forgotten resolutions.

### 🧩 Break It Down
Split big goals into smaller, manageable subgoals you can actually do. Tackle one step at a time and watch progress compound.

### 🖼️ Stay Motivated
Attach images to your goals to remind yourself *why* you started in the first place. A visual anchor for the moments you feel like quitting.

### 🔒 100% Private
Your data never leaves your device. No accounts. No servers. No tracking. Ever.

---

## Privacy

YourYear is built on a simple principle: **your goals are yours alone.**

- ✅ All data stored locally on your device
- ✅ No user accounts or sign-up required
- ✅ No analytics or usage tracking
- ✅ No internet connection needed
- ✅ No third-party data sharing
